This is a small gallery of artwork I have done in the past. I hope to keep working and improving my art. My biggest inspirations are movies, other artists, and nature. I love to draw animals and attempt many portraits.

Evertec challenge:

The Evertec Challenge was a competition hosted bu the Evertec company here in Puerto Rico. My team and I had to develop an app to help a food business manage inventory and orders. We were awarded second place in the competition.

RUM Air:

I am a member of the RUM Air team. Our goal this semester was to implement a Python GUI for the display of data received from an Arduino in the Advanced Class aircraft.
